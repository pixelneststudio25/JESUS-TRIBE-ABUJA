About Jesus Tribe

Who we are, what we believe, and why we exist

Our Story

Jesus Tribe began in 2021 as a small group of teenagers at Covenant Nation Church, Abuja, who were passionate about living out their faith authentically. What started as a weekly Bible study has grown into a vibrant community of over 100 young people aged 13-19.

We believe that teenagers are not just the church of tomorrow, but powerful contributors to the church today. Our mission is to create a safe space where teens can explore faith, build genuine friendships, and discover their purpose in Christ.

Our Core Values

Authentic Community

We prioritize real relationships where everyone is known, valued, and loved.

Biblical Foundation

God's Word is our guide for life, decisions, and spiritual growth.

Service & Outreach

We believe in putting our faith into action by serving others.

Joyful Worship

We express our love for God through contemporary worship relevant to teens.
